Between the brackets and the braces,
A coder finds familiar places,
Where logic flows like rivers wide,
And functions call from side to side.

The cursor blinks, the screen alight,
A thousand lines into the night,
Each semicolon, every loop,
A quiet dance, a silent swoop.

We build our worlds from nothing more
Than keystrokes on a wooden floor,
And in the hum of fans that spin,
We find the art that lives within.
